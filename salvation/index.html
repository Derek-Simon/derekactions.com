To derek:

What on earth is life about?

In my mind, life is all about achievements.
You accomplish your desires and expectations.
The time to complete your targets, or the journey which records your activities, is your life.

For example:
If you wanna a doctor’s degree, you need a master’s degree first, and to get master degree, you should be a graduate now.
If you wanna marry a young beauty who is well educated instead of just date, no marriage, you’d better make sure you have the ability to provide her a sweet and sufficient home, and the awareness of taking the responsibility as a husband.

If you look back to your life and you find nothing there, then you might have wasted your life.

To live a meaningful life or avoid being a loser, you have to commit your time and energy to certain targets in case of regret.
According to my past experiences, I think, you could do these mental exercises daily:

  • Keep a clear path to your long term and short term target (make a list)
  • (do)Track your time (to see whether you are moving forward, efficiently)
  • Keep positive and cheerful (try your best avoiding negative emotion which is really harmful)

Keeping writing what you did that day in your diary day to day is boring, however, things above to keep you full of confidence and aware of where you are and where to go, is quite necessary to do evey single day.

Hope you enjoy your life.
