Hold on to Project Management
Dec 31, 2021

This post is the Follow-up of my last observation and reflection: I am who I am。

0. Failed in turning

Almost a month (5 interviews/appointments) spent on finding an analysis job, only to find that I have made another a mistake.

After the last interview the day before yesterday(2021-12-29), I finally have to say: I basically have failed to make a turning from project manager to data analyst.

1. Stop fantasy, Face reality

Although I can go on trying, it’s of no necessity, ‘cuz the chance to data analysis for newbies/green-hands like me who worked for many years in other filed, is small.

If u can read in Chinese, then you could get to know the details by viewing the picture below.

Or there is one short summary:

If you’ve worked for a long time, like me, 5 years, then if you wanna find a simple, relax work with more leisure time and less salary to charge, it’s difficult. They would like to pay more to you to make u take more responsibilities instead of giving you more freedom to enjoy ur life, at least in the job market in Wuhan, China, I experienced.

Shrug.

2. Hobby VS Career

  • Analysis: Forcast, Assert/Deduce, Analyzing, Test and verify

Versus

  • Management: Lead people to complete a project and deliver/release it

Did u really take ur own occupation seriously?

Sorry, Forgive my mistake. I am sad about the time I wasted.

3. Manager for Global Markets

Or put it more specific: International Project Manager。

I Like solving with data as well as try new languages.

As I have been back to my old job path, I could make extensions to the managing group from the local chinese to the global team.

4. Action

Hold on to your own road.

Do one, Do well, Do now.

Right now.
