Intersection at 30 of my life
Feb 15, 2022

0 preface

Once in my conversation with my mom, she said without any conscious: what you can do with that your little salary/wage?
in Chinese: 你那点工资能干什么？

(her intention: if there are chance to run your business, then do not be a manager of others’)

Actually, the most common salary in Wuhan is 8~15K/month(below 20W/year)….

1 Conflict

Continue the career as a PM / Employee or be your own boss.

In fact, there are a benefit analysis:

Way Pros Cons
Employee identity as an employee at certain corporation, chance to know more over the world low income(10W/year), hard to overcome 30W RMB/year in Wuhan
Boss of my business 60W/year in sure, multi partners or relatives to solve my personal problems, the business is mature very very very busy, no relation to my life goal, more to care on my own

Once you become a boss of a traditional industry, then there almost no chance to go back as a normal employee.

The orientation of yourself(me) constructs the way to make a living or live a life.

This conclusion comes from my father’s word: even “Nobody” has his own way to live, so do not judge.

(Translate in Chinese: 小人物也有其生存之道，不懂就不要评价)

To be richer and richer / build your own commercial business ?

To be decent / get a dignified pay on the face ?

That is a confusing argument. I am not sure what is right or wrong.

2. Result

Except nearly 8W/year on the Investment/Trading/Finance Markets as a guarantee in my current stage, I’m not urged to make a decision in this latest time, while I may not be allowed to hesitate if I wanna go back to office, ‘cuz the recruitment market judges.

What a pity! (I can not obtain both income and decency)

What a shame! (money and time I both own, however I get confused where to go!)

I need make an intersection choice anyway.

(don’t push it too hard, I am not gonna uncover it at website)
