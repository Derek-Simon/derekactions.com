Feb 02, 2022

Where is Man’s Charm from?

uh.

  1. his calmness / less to talk, more to listen
  2. open mind, no comment & judgement, try to understand and encourage, respect
  3. his tune/song voice
  4. his body attraction
  5. his power,competence,career / occupation responsibility
  6. his richness
  7. his size

Man’s charm comes both from himself and what he possesses.

However, what really interests me is I find that once a guy puts his energy on his possessions, he would lose his charm gradually.

It is quite subtle to keep a balance between oneself and non-self.
